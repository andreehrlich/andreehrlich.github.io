Andre Ehrlich

I'm currently a PhD student in the Department of Statistics at the Athens University of Economics & Business, supervised by Nikos Demiris, and co-supervised by Petros Dellaportas.

I'm interested in understanding biomedical data via learning algorithms that operate on functional distributions. I am investigating how well Bayesian Gaussian Processes & Neural Diffusion Processes can (1) perform inference on dynamical epidemic models characterized by stochastic ordinary differential equations, and (2) extrapolate long-term survival probabilities for cancer data.
