About me

5+ years of industry experience building models, algorithms, and software for Internet Technology, Maritime Shipping, Biomedical & Health Science research.

Doctoral Candidate in Biostatistics at the Athens University of Economics & Business (AUEB). My thesis, "On the Analysis of Biomedical Data via Functional Distributions", is advised by Advised by Dr. Nikolaos Demiris (AUEB) & Dr. Petros Dellaportas (AUEB, UCL).

We are concerned with two distinct kinds of biomedical estimation problems: (1) epidemic forecasting and (2) survival extrapolation. We model the underlying processes as transformed Neural Diffusion and Gaussian Processes, and evaluate their efficiency (statistically, computationally) and extrapolation accuracy.
