I'm currently a PhD student in the Department of Statistics at the Athens University of Economics & Business, supervised by Nikos Demiris and co-supervised by Petros Dellaportas.

I'm working on understanding biomedical data through learning algorithms that operate on functional distributions. Methodologies of interest include Bayesian Gaussian Processes & Neural Diffusion Processes. I am interested in applying these methodologies to "real world" problems.

  1. Incorporating time-varying and individiual-level dynamics in stochastic ODE-driven epidemic models
  2. Extrapolating long-term survival probabilities for cancer treatment
